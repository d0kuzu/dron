TITLE: Battalion Reconnaissance Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS)
URL: https://api.sam.gov/prod/opportunities/v1/noticedesc?noticeid=1ae84253396340848943f71a741a1015
TEXT: <p>the u.s. army contracting command &ndash; redstone arsenal (acc-rsa) is issuing this call for solutions (abbreviated &ldquo;c4s&rdquo; hereafter) against commercial solutions opening (cso) w58rgz-26-s-c001 under the authority of 10 u.s.c. &sect; 3458. this c4s will competitively acquire innovative commercial, vertical take-off and landing (vtol) uncrewed aircraft systems (uas) for the battalion reconnaissance uas effort described below. the government reserves the right to alter or cancel this call at any time.</p>

<p>the purpose of this effort is to provide battalion commanders and their organizations with an organic reconnaissance, surveillance and target acquisition (rsta) capability. this effort aligns with the army&#39;s modernization priorities to operate in multi-domain operations (mdo) and the department of war&#39;s (dow) strategic objective of achieving drone dominance. through this c4s, the army ensures that industry remains informed of its changing needs, fostering ongoing investment and innovation in uas technologies to address future opportunities.</p>
